CHECK LOST ITEMS

  • Guidance on how to check lost items

    National Police Agency
    “Lost 112” use!

  • At Everland Resort, found lost items are registered and disclosed to Lost112, the National Police Agency's lost property search system, and lost items are kept for 6 months after they are found.

    Inquiries regarding lost property by phone (available during operating hours of each park)
    • - Everland Customer Service Center: 031-320-5050
    • - Caribbean Bay Information: 031-320-5085
  • If you have lost an item at Everland Resort, you can check if it was found through the Lost112 website or mobile app.
    Website for PC: www.lost112.go.kr Mobile APP: Search for "Lost112" Download
    in PLAY STORE or APP STORE
    [Notice]

    Food is discarded on the same day, perishable wet items and unusable items may be discarded within 2-3 days of acquisition. Expensive valuables are handed over to the Yongin Dongbu Police Station once a month, and mobile phones are handed over to the post office.
