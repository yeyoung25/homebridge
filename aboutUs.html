ABOUT US

Design Happiness

Everland Resort, whose name is a combination of “EVER,” meaning eternity and vitality, and “LAND,” which symbolizes nature and coziness, has been leading Korea’s leisure culture since its inception in 1976 as a “Nature Farm.”

Everland Resort's dry park, Everland, is a global theme park consisting of colorful festivals, attractions, zoos and botanical gardens. We provide the best pleasure to our customers with a variety of facilities and services, including five themed zones, various festivals that change every season, and the latest attractions.

festival
At Everland, which is open 365 days a year, festivals with different charms are held with different themes that make use of the characteristics of the four seasons. We provide joy to our customers with a new look every day through colorful entertainment, events, and special contents according to the festival.
attraction
More than 40 new attractions in Everland provide thrills and add to the fun. In particular, the wooden coaster T Express is considered the best attraction by roller coaster enthusiasts around the world, breaking various records such as the first in Korea and the world's best.
entertainment
Everland's super-sized entertainment boasting the highest level in Korea provides new pleasure every day, including a unique parade for each festival, various stage performances of various sizes held throughout the park, and a multimedia fireworks show.
Zoo
Everland Zoo 'Zootopia' exhibits about 2,000 animals of 200 species. You can experience high-quality animal experiences such as 'Safari World', 'Friendly Monkey Valley', a themed space dedicated to apes, and 'Lost Valley', Korea's first amphibious ecological safari.
Botanical garden
‘Plantopia’, a botanical garden composed of Everland’s flowers, trees, gardens and forests, combines the best landscaping and horticultural know-how of Everland, which has been leading flower festivals and garden culture in Korea for 40 years. We are realizing the satisfaction of the five senses through a themed garden that shows the beauty of plants, a new concept flower festival, and various experiential education programs.

Caribbean Bay, a water park, is not only the first water park in Korea, but also the world's largest indoor and outdoor water park. It is leading the water park trend with top-of-the-line facilities and various attractions themed on the Caribbean coast.

water attraction
From the world's largest artificial wave pool with a width of 120 meters and a length of 130 meters, to the world's longest lazy river, and various state-of-the-art slides. In particular, 'Mega Storm', which opened in 2015, is the largest water slide in Korea and provides refreshing and thrilling pleasure.
Spa and Beauty Zone
Caribbean Bay has a variety of spa facilities, making it an excellent family water play area even in winter. In addition, 'Beauty Zone' and 'Relax Room' reflecting the trend of well-being and beauty are operated indoors to provide aesthetic therapy and relaxation.
village
The Village, a bungalow-type resting space, is composed of various themes to suit the concept of each space. You can enjoy a comfortable rest along with pleasant water play.

Everland Resort has 'Home Bridge', an accommodation facility that claims to be a villa in nature. Homebridge is the main/new building of Cabin Hostel. It is composed of comfortable rest for guests.
