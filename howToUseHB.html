HOW TO USE

  • Everland 2-Day Pass Benefit

    - You may purchase an Everland 2-Day Pass at the front desk, which you can use from the arrival day at Home Bridge until the next day.

    - Adult KRW 72,000 / Children KRW 57,000

  • Precautions when using Homebridge
    • Homebridge prioritizes reservations for youth and student groups.
      Individual guests can make reservations from D-30 days based on the date of visit only if there are vacancies.
    • Homebridge Convenience Store does not sell cigarettes, but sells alcoholic beverages (beer).
    • Individual guests can make reservations from D-30 days based on the date of visit only if there are vacancies.
    • There is a separate smoking area, so please use the designated area.
    • Check-in is at 3pm and check-out is at 11am.
    • Even if you visit before check-in time (15:00) on the day of your stay, you can use the parking lot inside the cabin hostel.
    • * From the date of accommodation to the date of check-out, the parking lot is always available and no separate parking fee is charged
