THEME STORY

A place of joy and romance given by nature, A place with lush forests, fresh air, and fresh impressions from nature

Enjoy the leisure and romance of Alpine cottages with views over all of Everland
Home Bridge consists of the Cabin Hostel New wing and the Cabin Hostel main Building.
The following are the main features of each section of Home Bridge

  • CABIN HOSTEL NEW WING

    This is the Alps, drawn in a dream at an altitude of 2000 meters. Alog cabin with a quiet and still atmosphere, comfortable bed, and the best views of the scenery from the guest rooms.
    Create beautiful memories at the New Wing of Cabin Hostel, a small village inside the Alps.

  • CABIN HOSTEL MAIN BUILDING

    Take one or two steps out of your complicated daily life. Here, all the romance of nature awaits you, and it's not a stone’s throw from your place.
    Take a good rest together with your loved ones in a two-storey log cabin made from North American spruce wood.
