Traveling by Car
- Parking guide cast takes you to a nearby parking.
- Free shuttle irregularly operates between parking and main gate (strollers accommodated).
Guide to Parking Your Vehicle
- If the main gate parking is full, the parking staff (signboard) will guide you to the nearest parking in operation.
- Free shuttle irregularly operates between parking and main gate (takes about 5 minutes and strollers accommodated).
Guide to Valet Parking
- If you come to the MA parking lot, you can use the valet parking service that safely parks your vehicle and brings it to the place where you get off when you return home.
- Usage fee: 25,000 won per unit
- Operating hours: the same as the operating hours of Everland
- If the valet parking is full, please follow the staff's guidance and use the general parking lot.
- When there are many users for valet parking, waiting time to check-in might be delayed.
Regular bus
- For inquiries regarding reservations for regular tour buses, please contact the relevant travel agency.
- Some routes operate only for phone reservations or weekends.
- Please understand that the required time may change depending on bad weather and traffic conditions.
