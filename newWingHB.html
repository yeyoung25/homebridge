CABIN HOSTEL NEW WING

  • Here, you can stay within the Alpine idyll that you have
    always dreamed of, in a 2,000-meter high altitude of happiness.

    A log cabin with a quiet and still atmosphere, comfortable bed, and the best views of the scenery from the guest rooms.
    Create beautiful memories at the New Wing of Cabin Hostel, a small village inside the Alps.

    The Cabin Building is a luxurious log-built villa-style accommodation.

    The detached two-storey building provides visitors with private spaces on each floor, along with panoramic views of the beautiful natural setting through multiple windows.
