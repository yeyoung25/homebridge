RULES OF USE

Homebridge Hostel Rules
  • - Smoking and cooking are prohibited in all rooms.
  • - The use of electric heaters for heating or cooking purposes is prohibited in the guest rooms.
  • - Guest rooms cannot be used for purposes other than accommodation.
  • - Pets are not allowed in the room.
  • - Cabin hostel facilities, furnishings and furnishings cannot be used for any purpose other than the designated purpose, and cannot be taken out. In addition, if it is damaged or destroyed, you must compensate.
  • - Please refrain from bringing the following items into the cabin hostel.
    • - Ignition or inflammable toxic substances
    • - Items that can be used as weapons
  • - Cabin Hostel only helps to store items for guests who visit by public transportation.
    • - Individual guests who have their own car, please keep it.
  • - Cabin Hostel's lost and found items handling standards are as follows.
    1. ① When receiving a lost item, register it on the “National Police Agency LOST 112” site. National Police Agency Lost and Found Comprehensive Management System (lost112.go.kr)
    2. ② The lost item is transferred to the Everland Customer Service Center.
    3. ③ Other details regarding the handling of lost items follow the provisions of the Lost Property Act.
  • ※ In accordance with the law, customers specify the type and value of money, securities, and other high-value items. If not deposited, Cabin Hostel is not responsible for the loss or theft of the item.
  • - Please pay the fee to the receptionist at the front desk when making a reservation or when making a claim at the cabin hostel. Please note that if you do not comply with the above rules, you may refuse to stay or use the facilities in the cabin hostel and ask the customer to bear the responsibility.
  • - Except for staying guests, entry to Everland from the cabin hostel is restricted
  • ※ If a minor stays without a guardian, an Accommodation Agreement is required. Download the form PDF
    After filling out, please submit it when you visit. [Download Accommodation Agreement for Minors]
